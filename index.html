About Us

A different kind of vet visit.

Main Line Mobile Veterinary Services was founded on a simple belief: pets deserve exceptional care without the stress of a clinic. We bring everything your pet needs directly to your door.

Based in Chester Springs, PA, we serve all of Chester and Montgomery County. Travel is available upon request throughout the greater Main Line area.

What We Do

Full-spectrum care, at home.

From your pet's very first vaccine to compassionate end-of-life care — we're with you at every stage.

Wellness Exams

Comprehensive head-to-tail checkups for dogs and cats of all ages.

Puppy & Kitten Wellness

Start your new family member off right with early-life care packages.

Vaccinations

Core and lifestyle vaccines administered safely in your living room.

Routine Blood Work

Diagnostics to keep tabs on your pet's health between exams.

Telemedicine

Virtual vet visits when you need quick guidance without a house call.

Medical Services

Treatment for illnesses and injuries in the comfort of home.

Microchipping

Permanent ID for your pet — quick, safe, and done at home.

Online Pharmacy

Prescriptions and preventatives delivered directly to your door.

Nutritional Counseling

Personalized diet guidance for optimal pet health and longevity.

Compassionate End-of-Life Care

In-home euthanasia — a peaceful, dignified farewell in familiar surroundings, surrounded by family.

Personalized Wellness Plans

Preventive care plans tailored to your pet's life stage and needs.

The Mobile Difference

Your home is the best exam room.

Pets behave more naturally, show truer vital signs, and recover faster when examined at home. We see the real them — not a nervous version in a strange place.

The Team

The people behind the paw prints.

Dr. Sarah Overhiser, DVM

Founder & Director

Dr. Overhiser grew up in Wynnewood, PA and earned her undergraduate degree in Biology and Classical Studies from Bucknell University. She received her veterinary degree from Iowa State University in 2015, focusing on small animal preventative medicine and surgery. After working at practices in Berlin, MD and Phoenixville, PA, she attained her long-term goal of opening her own mobile veterinary clinic.

Donna

Veterinary Technician

Born and raised in Philadelphia, Donna received her Veterinary Technician degree from Harcum College in Bryn Mawr, PA. She enjoys spending time with her three pups — Rosie, Lilo & Buzz. Donna is the backbone of Main Line Mobile Vet and we are so thankful to have her as part of our team!
