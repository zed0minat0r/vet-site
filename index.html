About Us

A different kind of vet visit.

Main Line Mobile Veterinary Services was founded on a simple belief: pets deserve exceptional care without the stress of a clinic. We bring everything your pet needs directly to your door.

Based in Chester Springs, PA, we serve all of Chester and Montgomery County. Travel is available upon request throughout the greater Main Line area.

The Team

The people behind the paw prints.

Dr. Sarah Overhiser, DVM

Founder & Director

Iowa State University DVM 2015 Small Animal Medicine

Dr. Overhiser grew up in Wynnewood, PA and earned her undergraduate degree in Biology and Classical Studies from Bucknell University. She received her veterinary degree from Iowa State University in 2015, focusing on small animal preventative medicine and surgery. After working at practices in Berlin, MD and Phoenixville, PA, she attained her long-term goal of opening her own mobile veterinary clinic.

Donna

Veterinary Technician

Harcum College Vet Tech, Bryn Mawr PA

Born and raised in Philadelphia, Donna received her Veterinary Technician degree from Harcum College in Bryn Mawr, PA. She enjoys spending time with her three pups — Rosie, Lilo & Buzz. Donna is the backbone of Main Line Mobile Vet and we are so thankful to have her as part of our team!

Alex Torres

Veterinary Assistant

Penn Foster Vet Assistant Cert. Fear Free Certified

Alex joined the team with a passion for low-stress animal handling and a background in shelter medicine. A Fear Free certified assistant, Alex ensures every in-home visit is calm and comfortable for both pets and their owners.
